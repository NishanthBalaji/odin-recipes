Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice is a popular and flavorful dish featuring stir-fried rice mixed with tender chicken pieces, vegetables, and a blend of savory sauces. The dish typically includes ingredients like scrambled eggs, carrots, peas,and green onions, all cooked together in a hot wok.

Soy sauce, garlic, and ginger add depth to the flavor,making it a satisfying and delicious meal that can be enjoyed as a quick weeknight dinner or a special treat.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • Eggs
  • Mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, corn)
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the Chicken: Heat oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add diced chicken and cook until fully cooked and browned, then remove and set aside.
  2. Scramble the Eggs: In the same skillet, add a bit more oil if needed and pour in the beaten eggs. Scramble until fully cooked, then remove and set aside with the chicken.
  3. Cook Vegetables: Add mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn), chopped onion, and minced garlic to the skillet. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Combine Ingredients: Add the cooked rice to the skillet, followed by the cooked chicken and scrambled eggs. Pour in soy sauce and optionally oyster sauce, stirring everything together until well combined and heated through.
  5. Season and Serve: Drizzle with sesame oil, season with salt and pepper to taste, and stir well. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired, then serve hot.
