Honey Garlic Chicken Wings

Honey garlic chicken wings are a delightful combination of sweet and savory flavors, making them a favorite appetizer or snack. The wings are coated in a sticky honey garlic sauce that caramelizes as they bake, creating a crispy and flavorful outer layer.

Whether served as a party dish or enjoyed as a weeknight treat, these chicken wings are sure to please everyone's taste buds.

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Honey
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic cloves, minced
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Prepare the Sauce: In a saucepan, combine honey, soy sauce, minced garlic, and butter. Heat over medium heat until the butter is melted and the sauce is well combined.
  3. Coat the Chicken Wings: Place the chicken wings in a large bowl and season with salt and pepper. Pour the honey garlic sauce over the wings, tossing until they are evenly coated.
  4. Bake the Wings: Arrange the coated wings in a single layer on the prepared baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the wings are golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve Hot: Remove the wings from the oven and let them cool slightly before serving. Enjoy your honey garlic chicken wings with your favorite dipping sauce!
